Welcome to SpotGalaxy!

Just like our universe, your music taste is made up of a huge collection of stars, each of them chosen and loved by you. But sometimes, when your universe grows far beyond its old limits, some of these stars get lost, and forgotten. With SpotGalaxy, you can use machine learning algorithms to group your favorite songs into star clusters based on similar attributes like popularity score, age, and runtime, so you can rediscover all of your different music vibes. Note: The more clusters you make, the more accurate the groupings are, so choose wisely!
